Self Reflection / Reflective Essay

The Media and Information Literacy (MIL) course under Ms. Camille C. Cano was a challenging but valuable learning experience for me. One of the biggest struggles I faced was understanding the complexity of the media landscape and the various biases and perspectives present in different sources of information. However, Ms. Cano's approach of fostering class discussions and group projects helped me develop critical thinking skills and learn how to evaluate and analyze different sources of information.

Another challenge I faced was keeping up with the constant influx of information and staying current on current events. Ms. Cano's approach of incorporating current events into class discussions and assignments helped me to develop a deeper understanding of the material and become more aware of the issues happening in the world.

Overall, the MIL course under Ms. Cano's guidance has had a significant impact on me as a student. It has helped me become more media literate and better equipped to navigate the information landscape with a critical perspective. The class has also helped me to stay informed about current events and develop critical thinking skills.
